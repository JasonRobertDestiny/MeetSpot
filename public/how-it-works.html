How MeetSpot Works

This guide walks you through the exact workflow we use to push SEO-ready recommendation pages live: collect locations, calculate the midpoint, analyze venues, and publish the findings.

Step 1 · Collect & clean addresses

Add between two and ten locations. You can paste street addresses, landmarks, or coordinates. MeetSpot normalizes each entry, fills missing city names, and validates the coordinates against the AMap Places API.

Tip: for recurring meetings, store participant home bases in a CSV and import via the API.

Step 2 · Choose keywords & context

Select venue keywords like “meeting location midpoint”, “group meeting”, or “find midpoint coffee”. This ensures exported pages align with the SEO keyword set outlined in our optimization guide.

  • Primary keyword: meeting location midpoint
  • Secondary keywords: group meeting point finder, find midpoint between addresses
  • Localized keywords: 聚会地点推荐, 会面点

Step 3 · Calculate the midpoint & venues

3.1 We apply weighted centroid math to locate a fair midpoint.
3.2 The system fetches 20 venues within a configurable radius and ranks them by distance, rating, and your requirements.
3.3 MeetSpot renders a static HTML recap, complete with WebApplication, LocalBusiness, FAQ, and Review schema.

Step 4 · Publish & monitor

  1. Share the generated /workspace/js_src/*.html file with your team or embed it into Notion.
  2. Submit the URL through Search Console Inspect to trigger indexing.
  3. Track rankings via Keyword.com and review CTR in GA weekly.
