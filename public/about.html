About MeetSpot

MeetSpot began as a remote team ritual tool. Today it powers midpoint planning for startups, universities, and community groups in 30+ cities. We combine precise geodesic routing with a thoughtful SEO content strategy so every public page delivers value to humans and search engines alike.

Mission & Principles

Hybrid life is messy when everyone travels from different neighborhoods. MeetSpot’s mission is to keep gatherings equitable by recommending a midpoint that minimizes overall travel time. Every release must pass three filters: fairness of commute, clarity of communication, and measurability of SEO outcomes.

Fairness

Our midpoint calculations weigh distance, travel mode, and live congestion to keep each participant within ±15% of the average commute.

Clarity

We default to plain language, bilingual summaries, and schema markup so recommendation pages can be shared or indexed instantly.

Measurement

Every change ships with a KPI: natural traffic, conversion to booking, or structured data coverage. We monitor results through Search Console, GA, and Keyword.com.

Who we serve

MeetSpot supports product teams arranging quarterly syncs, HR leaders planning onboarding tours, alumni clubs revisiting favorite neighborhoods, and agencies co-planning with clients. 65% of customers operate in English-first markets, while 35% rely on our Chinese interface to plan 聚会地点 (gathering spots) around mainland cities.

SEO & Content Operations

We treat SEO as a product feature. Each public-facing recommendation comes with WebApplication + LocalBusiness schema, FAQ markup, and an internal link strategy that points back to How it Works, FAQ, and API docs. Trend analysis runs weekly via SerpAPI + Keyword.com to uncover emerging queries like “group meeting point finder” or “find midpoint between teams”. We adjust copy, internal links, and CTAs whenever click-through drops by more than 5%.
