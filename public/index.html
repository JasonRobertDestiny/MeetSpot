How it works

  1. Input addresses or landmarks: Add between two and ten locations. MeetSpot automatically normalizes coordinates.
  2. Describe the meeting: Specify venue type, accessibility notes, or preferred ambience.
  3. Review the ranked venues: We calculate the midpoint, fetch nearby venues, score them, and export an SEO-ready recap.

What makes our midpoint fair?

MeetSpot weights each participant by distance and travel mode, then applies a centroid algorithm adapted from logistics routing. The result is a midpoint that keeps everyone within ±15% of the average commute.

Every recommendation includes walking distance, estimated travel time, venue ratings, and quick links to share via email or calendar invites.

Quick FAQ

Is there a limit on venues?

Each request surfaces up to twenty venues ranked by fairness, rating, and proximity. You can regenerate results with different keywords at any time.

Does MeetSpot support Chinese interfaces?

Yes. 虽然页面以英文为主，但系统仍然支持中文地址输入与“聚会地点推荐”等关键词，方便本地团队使用。

Can I embed results?

The API returns a static HTML recap hosted under /workspace/js_src, ready to share publicly or embed into Notion, Confluence, or your CMS.

How do I monitor performance?

Connect Search Console and Keyword.com to track rankings for “meeting location midpoint”, “group meeting point finder”, “find midpoint”, and related phrases.
